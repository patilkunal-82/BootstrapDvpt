ReadARs

The platform where readers enjoy immersive reading experience by sharing & consuming engaging content and having meaningful conversations on books they have read or want to read next !!

Do you find yourself 'wanting more context & content' while reading books?

ReadARs - Enjoy immersive reading experience

Point your mobile phone at pre-defined and user generated markers in the book you are reading and contextual and relevant content (images, videos, 3D models et al) is rendered on your mobile screens. Enjoy curated content published by authors, publishers as well.

With immersive content and engaging rendering technique leveraging Augmented Reality(AR), you can now enjoy the contextual and immersive reading expereince.

Augmented Reality (AR) Portable Devices

Do you love 'contributing immersive content' so that others have enjoyable reading experiences?

ReadARs - Create immersive reading experience

Upload your experiences as media content (images, videos, 3D models et al) at pre-defined and user generated markers in the book you are reading.

With ability to share immersive content, you can now express yourself and let readers have contextual and immersive reading experiences.
